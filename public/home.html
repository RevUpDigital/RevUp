About RevUp

Helping local businesses turn great service into lasting reputation.

Google reviews have become one of the biggest factors in how customers choose where to spend their money. Whether it’s a tradie, clinic, or local service business, most people decide who to contact based on reputation and online ratings before they ever make a call.

RevUp was founded by two friends who noticed the same problem across local businesses everywhere — great work was being delivered, but not enough customers were turning that positive experience into online reviews.

So we built RevUp to fix it.

Our goal is simple: help service-based businesses grow their reputation without adding more admin or extra work to their day.

By automating review requests and simplifying customer feedback, RevUp helps businesses consistently generate more 5-star Google reviews while staying in control of the customer experience.

What started as a simple idea between two friends has grown into a platform designed to help local businesses compete and win online — without chasing customers or dealing with complicated systems.

Today, RevUp is focused on one thing: helping hardworking businesses turn great service into lasting reputation.

FAQs

Do I need technical setup?

No. Create your business, add your Google review link, and start sending SMS requests.

What happens when I hit my SMS limit?

SMS sending pauses until your monthly reset. Your dashboard shows how many SMS you have used.

Where does negative feedback go?

It is saved privately in your RevUp dashboard and emailed to the business owner.

Can I use my own Google review link?

Yes. Each business adds their own Google review link during signup.
